Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif adalah orang, binatang atau benda ditunjuk sebagai objek atau dikatakan menderita sesuatu dari sesuatu yang lain. Penjelasan:

Present a continuous can be used to tell of an event or activity that is done within a certain period of time and still ongoing. An example of a sentence in this function is the man is writing his novel this month. The sentence states that writing a novel is a common activity that the man should do this month, but it does not mean that when spoken of, he was in his room and writing a novel.

Another function of a present continuous is to tell of an event or an event that will take place in the near future or is already contemplating. The example of this is the woman is closing her store next two hours. The sentence states that the woman would close her store and what she was about to do that would happen in the near future and was certain.

7. Dalam bahasa Inggris, kata kerja transitif memiliki dua ragam gramatikal, yaitu aktif (active voice) dan pasif (passive voice). Kalimat aktif merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana orang, binatang atau benda yang ditunjuk sebagai subjek dikatakan melakukan sesuatu pada yang lain. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif adalah orang, binatang atau benda ditunjuk sebagai objek atau dikatakan menderita sesuatu dari sesuatu yang lain. Di dalam kehidupan sehari-hari, kalimat aktif lebih sering digunakan dibandingkan dengan kalimat pasif.Kalimat pasif dapat dibentuk dari tiga tenses utama dalam bahasa Inggris, yaitu present tense, past tense dan future tense. Pada artikel ini akan dijelaskan mengenai rumus serta contoh yang digunakan dalam membentuk kalimat pasif pada simple future tense. Penjelasan akan terbagi menjadi tiga, yakni kalimat pasif dalam bentuk kalimat positif, kalimat negatif serta kalimat pertanyaan.1. RUMUS DAN CONTOH KALIMAT POSITIFRumus: Subjek + will/shall + be + Verb III.Contoh: 1. I will give you a writing assignment in Japanese tomorrow. (Aktif) You will be given a writing assignment in Japanese tomorrow. (Pasif) 2. Security officer will ask to show our identification. (Aktif) We will be asked to show our identification by security officer. (Pasif)2. RUMUS DAN CONTOH KALIMAT NEGATIFRumus: Subjek + will/shall + not + be + Verb III.Contoh:1. He will meet them next Sunday at Tebet. (Aktif) They will not be met by him next Sunday at Tebet. (Pasif)2. The farmers will harvest the crops next month. (Aktif) The crops shall not be harvested by the farmers next month. (Pasif)3. RUMUS DAN CONTOH KALIMAT PERTANYAANRumus: Will/shall + subjek + be + Verb III.Contoh:1. They will buy a bike for her next week. (Aktif) They will not be met by him next Sunday at Tebet. (Pasif)2. The waitress will serve them. (Aktif) The crops shall not be harvested by the farmers next month. (Pasif)Ubahlah menjadi PASIF!1.Somebody will clean the room later.2.They will not use the computers an hour later.3.Some rich men are going to build a new supermarket here next year.4.Is she going to buy a new car next month?Ubahlah menjadi AKTIF!1.Their tasks will be finished soon.2.I will not be invited by her to the party.3.Some languages are not going to be learned by Bunga.4.Is this novel going to be returned?​


Passive voiceThe room will be cleaned (by somebody) later.The computers will not be used (by them) an hour later.A new supermarket is going to be built here later by some rich men.Is a new car going to be bought (by her) next month?

Active voiceThey will finish their task soon.She will not invite me to the party.Bunga is not going to learn some languages.Is anyone going to return this novel?

Preface

Dalam tata bahasa Inggris, sering digunakan dua jenis grammatical voice, yaitu:

active voice → subjek kalimat adalah pelaku aksipassive voice → subjek kalimat adalah yang dikenai aksi

Contoh:

Active: Mary eats meat → Mary melakukan aksi, yaitu memakanPassive: Meat is eaten by Mary → daging dikenai aksi, yaitu dimakan

Struktur umum kalimat aktif adalah:

Subject + verb

Struktur umum kalimat pasif adalah:

Subject + be + past participle

ーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Semua kalimat dalam soal menggunakan future tense, maka kalimat aktifnya berpola

Subject + will + base verb

atau

Subject + am/is/are + going to + infinitive

Kalimat pasif dalam future tense berpola

Subject + will + be + past participle

atau

Subject + is/am/are + going to + be + past participle
