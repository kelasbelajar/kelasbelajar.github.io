18. 1. Take an exam = ... Example: 2. Fail an exam = ... Example: .... 3. Cram = ... Example: 4. Learn by heart = .... Example: 5. Cheat = Example: ...​


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

1. Take an exam = mengambil ujian

Example: He takes an exam at the university every year.

2. Fail an exam = gagal dalam ujian

Example: She sometimes fails an exam.

3. Cram = belajar sungguh-sungguh

Example: David always crams for his final tests.

4. Learn by heart = menghafal

Example: She usually learns it by heart.

5. Cheat = menyontek

Example: We do not cheat in our exams.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan fakta dan aktivitas kegiatan berulang sehari hari dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat Kalimatnya:

1. Take an exam = mengambil ujian

Example: He takes an exam at the university every year. (Dia (cowok) mengambil ujian di universitas setiap tahun)

2. Fail an exam = gagal dalam ujian

Example: She sometimes fails an exam. (Dia (cewek) kadang-kadang gagal dalam ujian)

3. Cram = belajar sungguh-sungguh

Example: David always crams for his final tests. (David selalu belajar sungguh-sungguh untuk ujian akhirnya)

4. Learn by heart = menghafal

Example: She usually learns it by heart. (dia (cewek) biasanya menghafalkannya)

5. Cheat = menyontek

Example: We do not cheat in our exams. (kami tidak menyontek dalam ujian kami)

Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every year)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every year)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every year) + ?
