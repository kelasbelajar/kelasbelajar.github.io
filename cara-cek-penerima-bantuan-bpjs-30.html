Pembahasan

Bilangan Berpangkat adalah sebuah bilangan yang ditujukan untuk menyederhanakan penulisan bilangan apabila dikali dengan angka yang sama dengan berulang-ulang kali.

Perpangkatan adalah sebuah bentuk operasi dalam ilmu matematika yang boleh digunakan apabila sebuah bilangan dikali dengan bilangan yang sama lebih dari satu kali perkalian.

✏ ️Contoh Bilangan Perangkatan 2 Yaitu :

4 × 4 = 4².

Dibaca empat pangkat lima. Kenapa ? Karena 4 nya itu dikali kan sebanyak lima kali. Dan jadi lah Dibaca empat pangkat dua atau empat kuadrat [4²].

✏️ Contoh Bilangan Perpangkatan 3 Yaitu :

2 × 2 × 2 = 2³.

Dibaca dua pangkat tiga. Kenapa ? Karena 2 nya itu dikali kan sebanyak tiga kali. Dan jadilah Dibaca dua pangkat tiga [2³].

✏️ Akar Pangkat Dua Yaitu :

n × n = g, Maka, √g = n.

√g dibaca akar pangkat dua atau akar kuadrat dari g.

✏️ Akar Pangkat Tiga Yaitu :

Hasil akar pangkat tiga dari suatu bilangan ialah bilangan yang jika dipangkatkan tiga menghasilkan bilangan di dalam akar tersebut.

³√125.

Dibaca Akar Pangkat Tiga dari bilangan 125 tersebut.

31. explain everything about health insurance (BPJS)-tolong cepat ​ poin 30


Health insurance or BPJS (Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Sosial) is a program implemented by the Indonesian government to provide affordable healthcare services for all Indonesian citizens. The program is aimed at improving the health and well-being of the Indonesian population, particularly those who cannot afford to pay for healthcare services on their own.

BPJS is a social security program that covers various aspects of healthcare, including medical treatment, hospitalization, surgery, and medications. The program is managed by the BPJS Health Agency, which is responsible for collecting contributions from its members and reimbursing healthcare providers for services rendered to its members.

To enroll in BPJS, an individual must first register as a member by paying a monthly premium, which varies depending on their income level. Members who are employed are required to contribute 5% of their salary, while self-employed individuals are required to pay a fixed premium based on their income level.

Once enrolled, BPJS members can access healthcare services at any BPJS-accredited healthcare provider, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. The program covers a wide range of medical conditions and treatments, including chronic diseases, emergency care, and maternity care.

One of the advantages of BPJS is that it provides access to healthcare services for all Indonesians, regardless of their financial situation. Additionally, BPJS offers a range of benefits, including access to specialist care, preventative healthcare services, and prescription medications.

However, BPJS has faced several challenges in recent years, including a shortage of healthcare providers, limited access to specialist care, and high out-of-pocket costs for some treatments. To address these challenges, the Indonesian government has launched several initiatives aimed at improving the quality of healthcare services provided through the BPJS program.

Overall, BPJS is an important social safety net for millions of Indonesians, providing access to affordable healthcare services and helping to improve the overall health and well-being of the Indonesian population.
