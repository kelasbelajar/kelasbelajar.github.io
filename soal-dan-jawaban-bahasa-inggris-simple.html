9. soal bahasa Inggris simple tense of be​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Bentuk spelling kata kerja pada orang ketiga (She, He, It), tergantung dari akhiran kata kerja (verb) tersebut.

Untuk kata kerja yang berakhiran -O, -CH, -SH, -X, atau -Z, tambahkan -ES.

go – goes

catch – catches

wash – washes

kiss – kisses

box – boxes

fix – fixes

Untuk kata kerja (Verb) yang berakhiran konsonan + Y, hilangkan Y, dan tambahkan -IES.

carry – carries

study – studies

worry – worries

marry – marries

Sedangkan, untuk kata kerja yang berakhiran vokal + Y, cukup tambahkan -S.

play – plays

say – says

enjoy – enjoys

-The President of Indonesia is Joko Widodo.

-A snake has no legs.

-Indonesia has thirty-four provinces, from Sabang to Merauke.

3.Untuk menunjukkan suatu kebiasaan (habit).

-Doni gets up early at 5 o’clock every day.

10. apa itu simple past dan apa itu continuous? dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban :

- Simple Past -

The simple past tense, sometimes called preterite, is used to talk about an event that was finished at a time before the present. The simple past is the basic form of the past tense in English. Events can be recent in the past or long in the past, and the duration of their occurrence is not important.

- Present Continous Tense -

Present continuous tense is a tense that functions to express an action that is being done in the present (when speaking) and something in the future. The continual present is formed by the auxiliary verb "to be" such as am, is, and is followed by the present participle (V1 + ing).
