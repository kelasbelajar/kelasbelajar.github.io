31. bagaimana rumus question tag?


lingking verb"be"/auxiliary verb +/-not+pronoun
note:
----> lingking verb "be" (yaitu:is,am,was,were,are)
----> auxiliary verb berupa primary auxiliary verb (be,do,have) atau modal auxiliary verb(will, would, may, might,can, could, shall, should) yg cocok dg verb bagian pernyataan
-----> pronoun yg cocok dg subject bagian pernyataan.
example: you love sains, don't you?

37. Question tag .........? Lengkapilah dengan question tag


Answers

1. Your name is Salma, isn't it?

2. Those men spoke English very well, didn't they?

3. You and I are friends, aren't we?

4. That car moves very fast, doesn't it?

5. Rini's boy friends come here, don't they?

6. My parents won't go to Bali, will they?

7. Marisa has come here on time, hasn't she?

8. They should study hard now, shouldn't they?

9. Father reads newspaper every morning, doesn't he?

10. We don't always go to the swimming pool every Sunday, do we?

---------

Explanation

Question tag adalah sebuah klausa interogatif singkat yang diletakkan di ujung sebuah pernyataan/perintah. Question tag mengubah sebuah pernyataan menjadi pertanyaan dan sebuah kalimat perintah menjadi permintaan.

Question tag biasanya dipakai untuk meminta penegasan atas suatu pernyataan.

Question tag selalu berlawanan dengan kalimat utamanya.

Jika kalimat utama positif, maka tag negatif, dan sebaliknya.

Konstruksi Question Tag adalah : kata kerja bantu/modal + subject pronoun.

Jika tagnya negatif, kata kerja bantu+not selalu dikontraksi (disingkat).

Pembahasan

Question tag adalah sebuah pertanyaan kecil di akhir kalimat. Biasanya digunakan untuk menanyakan tentang persetujuan atau konfirmasi.


Kalimat positif + question tag negatif

(+) You are a student, (-) aren’t you?


Berikut adalah beberapa contoh question tag lainnya:

He is very clever, isn’t he?She was happy, wasn’t she?They were surprised, weren’t they?You speak French, don’t you?He studies Biology, doesn’t he?You will pass the exam, won’t you?You could do it for me, couldn’t you?

Pengecualian

I am late, aren’t I?Let’s go home, shall we?

Kalimat negatif + question tag positif

(-) Jane isn’t a doctor, (+) is she?


Berikut adalah beberapa contoh question tag lainnya:

He isn’t very clever, is he?She wasn’t happy, was she?They weren’t surprised, were they?You don’t speak French, do you?He doesn’t study Biology, does he?You will not pass the exam, will you?You could not do it for me, could you?

Catatan

Kalimat yang menggunakan kata barely, hardly, neither, no, never, nobody, none, nothing, seldom dianggap sebagai kalimat negatif.

Nobody went to the meeting, did they?Nothing is ready, is it?
