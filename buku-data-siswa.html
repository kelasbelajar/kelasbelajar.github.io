P(A) = 55

P(B) = 70

P(AnB) = 30

P'(AnB) =...?

P(AuB) = P(A) + P(B) + P'(AnB) - P(AnB)

120 = 55+70 + P'(AnB) - 30

120 = 95 +P'(AnB)

25 = P'(AnB)
